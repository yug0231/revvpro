FAQs

What are my liabilities in case of damage / accident of the Mahindra car?

Your Mahindra car will be covered by comprehensive insurance, to take care of damage cases. On every case of damage, your liability would be limited to the difference between the costs incurred to repair the damage and the proceeds from insurance claim with maximum liability limited to INR 10,000 In situations of irresponsible or unsafe or negligent usage, there could be discretionary exceptions to this policy, whereby you could be liable for all damages irrespective of whether or not we are able to claim insurance in such cases.

You can use the Mahindra car for all the normal requirements. However, abnormal usage like speed racing, towing/pushing/propelling, transportation of goods or people on commercial basis, transportation of large/heavy/hazardous items are not allowed.
